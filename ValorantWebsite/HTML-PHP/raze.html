RAZE

About

Raze loves explosives. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of "boom".



Abilities

  • Basics

    • BOOM BOT

    Equip a Boom Bot. Fire to deploy the bot, causing it to travel in a straight line on the ground, bouncing off walls. The Boom Bot will lock on to any enemies in its frontal cone and chase them, exploding for heavy damage if it reaches them.


    BLAST PACK

    Instantly throw a Blast Pack that will stick to surfaces and begin arming over Duration.png0.5 sec. Re-use to detonate, damaging and knocking back anything hit. Shooting the pack will also cause it to detonate.


  • Signature

    • PAINT SHELLS

    Equip a cluster grenade. Fire to throw the grenade, which deals damage and creates sub-munitions, each exploding and dealing further damage.


  • Ultimate

    • SHOWSTOPPER

    Equip a single-use Rocket Launcher and light its fuse. Fire to launch a rocket, which travels in a straight line and explodes on contact, dealing high damage to enemies caught in its blast radius.
